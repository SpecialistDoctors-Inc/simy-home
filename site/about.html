About SIMY

We believe the best code starts with great conversations — and that shipping it shouldn't require a single prompt.

"Every meeting should end with code that ships."

That's the future we're building at AwakApp Inc.

The Problem

Engineering teams spend 30% of their time in meetings — aligning on architecture, reviewing designs, triaging bugs. But after the meeting ends, the real work begins: translating those decisions into code, pull requests, and reviews. That translation layer is where velocity dies.

Existing AI coding tools require prompts, context-switching, and manual orchestration. They help you write code faster, but they don't eliminate the gap between decision and delivery.

Our Solution

SIMY listens to your team's natural conversations and automatically generates production-ready code, reviews, and pull requests. No prompts. No rework. Just record your meeting, and watch code ship.

By treating conversation as the interface, SIMY removes the translation layer entirely — turning every meeting into a deployment event.
