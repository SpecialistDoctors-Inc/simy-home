06 / Philosophy

Great Work Starts with
Great Conversations

Decisions Ship Immediately

When a meeting decides what to do, execution begins. SIMY's goal is to bring the time between decision and delivery as close to zero as possible. Humans should focus on the top 10% of critical matters that generate 90% of results. Everything else should be handled by the system.

High-Purity User Focus

People may look at the same thing, but they don't understand it the same way. When someone interprets and summarises what was decided, intent gradually drifts. SIMY minimises that drift by generating output directly from the original conversation — preserving context, nuance, and the 'why' behind every decision.

Conversation as Interface

When humans think deeply and make good decisions, much of it happens in conversation. Writing code, drafting documents, running analyses — these require a different kind of focus. We want to eliminate the constant mode-switching. Humans think, dialogue, and decide. SIMY connects that to reliable execution.

09 / FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of work can SIMY handle?
SIMY handles any knowledge work — strategy documents, research reports, data analysis, marketing copy, financial modelling, legal drafts, and more. If it can be described in a meeting or conversation, SIMY can execute it.
Who is SIMY designed for?
SIMY is designed for everyone from listed enterprises to SMEs to individual operators. Any team or person who wants to delegate execution to AI and focus on judgment will benefit.
Do I need to write prompts?
No. SIMY extracts requirements, specs, and decisions directly from your meeting recordings and team conversations. You never need to write a prompt — just have your normal meetings and SIMY handles the rest.
What meeting and communication tools are supported?
SIMY integrates with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Slack huddles, Google Meet, and Gmail threads. Any recorded conversation or written communication can be used as input.
How does SIMY protect my data?
Every organisation gets a dedicated, isolated database instance. On the Enterprise plan, your proprietary data is never used for training global models. SIMY uses end-to-end encryption and physical database isolation.
