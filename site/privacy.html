4. How We Use Your Information (AI Training Policy)

Our use of User Content for AI development depends on your subscription plan:

  • (a) Standard and Pro Plans (Individual): We may use User Content and voice transcriptions to train, fine-tune, and improve our AI models and machine learning algorithms.
