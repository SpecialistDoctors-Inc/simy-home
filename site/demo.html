Taken verbatim from the SIMY Home page — persona 04 of 9. The Product Manager at a 150-person tech company. No name, just the role and the story.

Before SIMY

Planning ends. Real work begins.

After every planning session, someone spends hours turning discussion into a structured roadmap. Decisions get lost. Owners get fuzzy. The doc lands in Notion by Thursday.

With SIMY

Meeting ends. Roadmap ships.

SIMY generates a structured roadmap, assigns owners, and sends summaries automatically. Every decision is captured the moment it's made — no cleanup required.

The outcome

Roadmap ready
before the next standup.

Her squads show up to standup with a shared, written plan — not a half-remembered meeting. Mondays are now about questions, not note-taking.
