Frequently Asked Questions

What are tokens?

Tokens are the currency used by SIMY's AI engine. Each code generation task, review, and pull request consumes tokens based on complexity. Larger tasks use more tokens.

Do tokens expire?

Tokens purchased in USD never expire. However, tokens purchased in Japanese Yen (JPY) expire 6 months after purchase in accordance with Japan's Payment Services Act.

How many tokens does a typical task use?

A simple code generation task from email or chat typically uses less than 1M tokens. Meeting-based generation, including transcription, uses around 10M tokens. Complex multi-file refactoring with tests and reviews may use 10–30M tokens.

Can I buy more tokens anytime?

Yes. Purchase additional token packages anytime from within the SIMY app. Tokens from multiple purchases are combined into your balance.

Is my data used for AI training?

On our Enterprise plan, your proprietary data and code are never used for training global models. Your intellectual property stays yours.
