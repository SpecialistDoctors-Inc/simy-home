Frequently Asked Questions

What are SIMY tokens?

Tokens are the currency used by SIMY's AI engine. Each code generation task, review, and pull request consumes tokens based on complexity. Your plan includes a monthly token allocation that resets each billing cycle.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll receive the additional tokens immediately. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I run out of tokens?

If you exhaust your monthly allocation, you can purchase additional token packs or upgrade your plan. Your existing work and data remain fully accessible.

Do unused tokens roll over?

Monthly token allocations reset each billing cycle and do not roll over. For maximum flexibility, consider the Scale plan or contact us about Enterprise options.

Is annual billing available?

Yes. Choose annual billing to save 17% compared to monthly pricing. Annual plans are billed upfront for the full year.
