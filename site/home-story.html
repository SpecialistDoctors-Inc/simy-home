Yoshi is a Principal Product Manager at CoreWeave, leading CKS and Compute Abstractions. His week is roadmap reviews, customer escalations, and exec readouts — and the night shift of turning every discussion into PRDs, tickets, and decisions before standup.

Before SIMY

Roadmap review ends. Night shift begins.

After the CKS roadmap review, Yoshi would spend the evening rebuilding the conversation: what got decided, which migration date stuck, who owns the PRD. Half the "must decide" items would slip to next week.

With SIMY

Meeting ends. Actions are waiting.

SIMY listens through the review, captures every "must decide" item, drafts the five actions Yoshi needs to take next, and updates the CKS growth dashboard — before he's left the room.

The outcome

Digital Twin answers
"what should I do next?"

By the time Yoshi opens SIMY the next morning, the actions are ranked, the metrics are updated, and his Digital Twin already knows which action moves CKS adoption the most. No more night shift.
